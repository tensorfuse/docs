Whip lets you describe an app in plain words, watch AI build it, tweak it just by chatting, then publish and share it with everyone. Here's everything we've shipped, week by week, since day one.

<Update label="Week of Jun 29, 2026" tags={["New", "Home"]}>
  ### ✨ New & improved

  - **Edit your Home screen like your phone's.** 🎉 Press and hold to enter
    edit mode, drag apps around with satisfying wiggle-and-react physics,
    drop one app onto another to make a folder, and tidy up with polished
    folder controls. Organizing your library finally feels like second
    nature. [How it works](/using/home-screen-and-folders).
  - **A long-press menu for every Home app** — open, share, remove, and more
    from one context menu, with a cleaner flow for removing apps from Home.
  - **An empty Home is no longer empty** — brand-new? Whip now suggests
    popular apps so your library starts with something to play.
  - Smoother loading everywhere — polished loading screens for Home, the
    feed, and app details instead of blank screens.

  ### 🐛 Fixed

  - Fixed rare crashes during app playback and a case where quickly editing
    the same app twice could show an "unable to save" error.
</Update>

<Update label="Week of Jun 22–28, 2026" tags={["New", "Feed", "Games"]}>
  ### ✨ New & improved

  - **Leaderboards.** 🎉 Game-style apps can now keep a real high-score
    board — it tracks each player's personal best, and Whip notifies you the
    moment someone beats your score. Bragging rights, formalized.
    [Play together](/using/play-together).
  - **Filter your feed by category** — chips for Gaming, Entertainment,
    Sports & Fitness, and the rest now sit right in the
    [feed](/discover/the-feed), which also moved its tabs up into the header
    for more room to scroll.
  - **A redesigned Home library** — new views for your collection: My Home
    (your custom order), Recently Played, Added, Alphabetical, By Creator,
    and By Category.
  - **Folders, properly** — create folders exactly where you want them on
    Home, drag-and-drop friendly.
  - **See a remix's story in the feed** — tap into any remix to see what it
    was remixed from, with a link back to the original.
  - Sharper app icons (better subjects, cleaner backgrounds) and a simpler,
    shorter onboarding flow.

  ### 🐛 Fixed

  - Fixed the follow button state on suggested-creator cards and smoothed
    out chat screen transitions.
</Update>

<Update label="Week of Jun 15–21, 2026" tags={["New", "Multiplayer", "Search"]}>
  ### ✨ New & improved

  - **Play together in shared sessions.** 🎉 Until now, everyone got their
    own private copy of every app. Now an app's owner can invite up to 10
    people into one live, shared copy — same list, same scoreboard, same
    data, updating for everyone. Shared grocery lists, party games, trip
    planners: [multiplayer has arrived](/using/play-together).
  - **Search and Explore, rebuilt** — one smarter search across apps,
    builders, and categories, tag-aware matching, and a proper Explore
    landing experience. Finding your next favorite app got much easier.
    [Take the tour](/discover/search).
  - **Highlight reels are ready sooner** — they now start generating the
    moment your app finishes building, so they're waiting for you at the
    [publish screen](/sharing/highlight-reels).
  - **Sharing an already-published app skips straight to sharing** — no
    redundant re-publish step.
  - App of the Day now arrives for everyone at 10 AM local time, custom app
    icons now show in notifications, a heart animation plays when you like
    an app, and Instagram shares include a preview image.

  ### 🐛 Fixed

  - Apps you haven't opened in a while now wake up automatically instead of
    failing to load.
  - Publishing right after an edit no longer races your autosave.
  - Fixed handling of non-English characters in apps that save data.
</Update>

<Update label="Week of Jun 8–14, 2026" tags={["New", "Builder"]}>
  ### ✨ New & improved

  - **Apps can connect to the internet.** 🎉 Generated apps can now fetch
    live data — weather, currency rates, trivia, and more from a curated set
    of safe sources (with your consent). Live scores and real-time info,
    unlocked. [What apps can do](/building/what-apps-can-do).
  - **Custom app icons everywhere on mobile** — across the feed, profiles,
    chat, and launch screens.
  - **"Discover great creators"** — a suggested-creators screen when you
    join Whip.
  - **A fairer App of the Day** — now chosen by real engagement in recent
    windows, not just recency.
  - Video support in feed cards, share links that open straight to the right
    app, and [notification settings](/social/notifications) to control
    exactly what Whip tells you about.
  - Fewer wrongly-blocked posts — a second-opinion safety check means
    legitimate content is less likely to be flagged by mistake.

  ### 🐛 Fixed

  - Hardened the AI against jailbreak attempts and explicit-content tricks.
  - Fixed email unsubscribe links and the re-subscribe flow.
  - Fixed a crash when uploading multiple files with the same name, plus an
    iOS rendering glitch.
</Update>

<Update label="Week of Jun 1–7, 2026" tags={["New", "Publishing"]}>
  ### ✨ New & improved

  - **Custom-designed app icons.** 🎉 Every app now gets a unique,
    art-directed icon made just for it — created when you build and when you
    publish. Generic icons, retired.
  - **Faster, more reliable highlight reels** — generation is about twice as
    fast, handles graphics-heavy apps, and shows live capture progress.
  - **A notifications & email overhaul** — weekly recap, trending, and
    feature-drop emails with an easy unsubscribe/resubscribe page.
  - Reorder your [highlight reel](/sharing/highlight-reels) with
    drag-and-drop, share to Instagram via a new image viewer, browse a
    Remixes tab on app sheets, and see published dates on feed cards.

  ### 🐛 Fixed

  - Failed edits no longer wipe your app's description or icon, and a single
    failure no longer breaks the rest of your version history (we also
    repaired 90+ affected apps).
  - Your feed now correctly reflects who you follow, thumbnails work for
    graphics-heavy apps, and a frozen "Capturing highlights" progress bar
    got unstuck.
  - Privacy fix: making a version private could still expose its old
    comments — now fixed.
</Update>

  ### 🐛 Fixed

</Update>

<Update label="Week of Apr 20–26, 2026" description="Whip 1.0" tags={["New", "Privacy"]}>
  ### ✨ New & improved

  - **Per-app permission consent.** 🎉 Apps must now ask before using your
    location, camera, microphone, or motion sensors — and you can review and
    revoke these permissions anytime in Settings → Permissions. Built with
    safeguards so an app can't impersonate a real system prompt.
    [How permissions work](/using/app-permissions).
  - **Whip 1.0** — the version that went out to the world.
  - **Clearer limits** — hitting a build limit now shows a clear message,
    keeps your partial work, and lets you pick up where you left off.
  - Remixed apps are now labeled, so you can tell remixes from originals.
  - Cleaner app thumbnails, and Terms & Privacy links on the sign-in,
    Settings, and account-deletion screens.

  ### 🐛 Fixed

  - Comment posting was failing about half the time in production — fixed.
  - You're no longer re-asked for permissions you already granted, and
    Android permission checks are reliable.
</Update>

<Update label="Week of Mar 30 – Apr 5, 2026" tags={["New", "Builder"]}>

  ### 🐛 Fixed

  - **Builds are about 3.5× faster** — fixed an issue that made every build

  - Finished apps no longer occasionally show as "failed", long active

  - Privacy: fixed an issue that mislocated users to the server's region.
</Update>
