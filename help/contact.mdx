The quickest way to reach Whip is email: [support@whip.run](mailto:support@whip.run). For fast questions, the [Whip Discord](https://discord.com/invite/bsSeNMQkTZ) is even quicker — the founders hang out there. You can also find Whip on social, and safety or urgent reports have their own fast path below.

## Email us

For anything account-related, a bug, or a question the docs didn't answer, email [support@whip.run](mailto:support@whip.run). A few common ones have quick self-serve answers already — like [managing your account](/account/manage-your-account) or a forgotten password.

<Tip>
  To get a fast, useful reply, include your **handle** (@username), a short
  description of **what happened**, and **screenshots** if you have them. That
  saves a round of back-and-forth and gets you unstuck sooner.
</Tip>

## Join the Discord

The [Whip Discord](https://discord.com/invite/bsSeNMQkTZ) is the fastest place for quick questions — the founders are there, alongside a community of builders sharing what they've made. Drop in to ask something small, show off an app, or get inspired.

## Which channel should I use?

Both work — pick whatever fits the moment. The Discord is best for quick,
in-the-flow questions and swapping ideas with other builders. Email is best
for anything tied to your account, a bug you want on record, or anything you'd
rather keep private. Before either, a quick look at
[troubleshooting](/help/troubleshooting) often solves the most common issues
on the spot.

## Safety and urgent reports

If you've seen content or behavior that breaks the rules, the fastest route is Whip's in-app reporting — it goes straight to the team for review.

<Warning>
  For anything time-sensitive, use [in-app reporting](/account/report-content)
  on the app, person, or comment. You can also email
  [support@whip.run](mailto:support@whip.run) with **"Report"** in the
  subject line. If you believe someone is in immediate danger, contact your
  local emergency services.
</Warning>
