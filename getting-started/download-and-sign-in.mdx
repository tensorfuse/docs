Whip is a free app for iPhone and Android. Get it from the App Store or Google Play (or visit [whip.run](https://whip.run) to grab the right link), open it, and sign in with Apple, Google, or an email and password. The whole thing takes under a minute, and you'll land straight in [onboarding](/getting-started/set-up-your-profile).

## Get the app

Whip lives on your phone — there's no desktop version. Download it from your device's store below.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="iOS">
    On iPhone, open the App Store and search for **Whip - Feed for AI
    Creations**, or go straight to the [App Store
    listing](https://apps.apple.com/in/app/whip-feed-for-ai-creations/id6758931714).
    Tap **Get** to install. Whip is an iPhone app — there's no separate iPad
    version.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Android">
    On Android, open Google Play and search for **Whip**, or open the [Play
    Store listing](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=run.whip.app)
    directly. Tap **Install**, then **Open**.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Note>
  Not sure which store you need? Head to [whip.run](https://whip.run) from your
  phone and it'll point you to the right download.
</Note>

## Sign in or create an account

Open Whip and you'll see the sign-in screen. You have three ways in — pick whichever you like.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Accept the Terms" icon="square-check">
    Tick the checkbox to agree to Whip's [Terms of
    Service](https://whip.run/terms) and [Privacy
    Policy](https://whip.run/privacy). You'll need this checked before you can
    continue.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose how to sign in" icon="key">
    Tap **Continue with Apple**, **Continue with Google**, or use your **email
    and password**. Apple and Google are the fastest — one tap and you're in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="If you're using email" icon="mail">
    Enter your email and a password with **at least 10 characters**. Whip sends
    a **6-character verification code** to your inbox — type it in to confirm
    it's you. The code **expires after 10 minutes**, so grab it while it's
    fresh. If it doesn't arrive, check your spam folder.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Whip is for adults. You'll be asked for your date of birth during setup, and
  you need to be **18 or older** to use the app. More on this in [age
  requirements](/account/age-requirements).
</Warning>

## What if I forget my password?

There's no self-serve password reset in Whip today. If you signed up with email and can't get back in, email **support@whip.run** and the team will help you sort it out. If you originally signed in with **Apple** or **Google**, there's no Whip password to forget — tap that same button again to get back in.
