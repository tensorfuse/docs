New to Whip, or deciding whether to download it? These are the questions everyone asks first — with straight answers. For build-specific questions head to the [creating apps FAQ](/faq/creating-apps), and for data questions the [privacy & safety FAQ](/faq/privacy-and-safety).

## The basics

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is Whip?" icon="sparkles">
    Whip is a free mobile app where you describe an app idea in plain words
    and AI builds it into a real, working mini app — usually in about a
    minute. You can then edit it by chatting, keep it private, publish it to
    a community feed, share it with friends, or remix other people's
    creations. The full tour lives at [What is Whip?](/).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is Whip free?" icon="badge-dollar-sign">
    Yes — completely. There are no subscriptions, no in-app purchases, and
    nothing to unlock. Building has [daily
    limits](/building/build-limits-and-failed-builds) that are generous
    enough that most people never notice them, and they reset on their own.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Which phones does Whip work on?" icon="smartphone">
    iPhone and Android. Grab it from the [App Store or Google
    Play](/getting-started/download-and-sign-in). There's no web or desktop
    version — Whip lives on your phone, and the apps you make run right
    inside it.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do I need to know how to code?" icon="code">
    No — that's the whole point. You describe what you want ("a plant
    watering reminder with a cute frog mascot") and the AI writes all the
    code. You never see a line of it unless you go looking.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How old do I need to be?" icon="user-check">
    18 or older. Whip checks your age during sign-up, and under-18 accounts
    can't be created. Details in [age
    requirements](/account/age-requirements).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Who makes Whip?" icon="building">
    Whip is built by Tensorfuse, Inc. You can find the team in the [Whip
    Discord](https://discord.com/invite/bsSeNMQkTZ) and on X, TikTok, and
    YouTube at @joinwhip.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Making and owning things

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What can I actually build?" icon="wand-sparkles">
    Trackers, calculators, games (with sound, effects, and leaderboards),
    quizzes, journals, AI companions with their own chat, tools that pull
    live weather or currency rates — see [what apps can
    do](/building/what-apps-can-do) for the full toolbox and
    [prompt ideas](/building/prompt-tips-and-ideas) for inspiration.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How long does it take to build an app?" icon="timer">
    Usually about a minute or two. Ambitious, multi-feature apps can take
    longer. You don't have to watch — Whip notifies you when it's ready.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do other people see what I make?" icon="lock">
    Not unless you choose. Every app starts **private** — only you can open
    it. It becomes visible to others only when you
    [publish it](/sharing/publish-your-app) or send someone a
    [share link](/sharing/share-links).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Who owns the apps I make?" icon="badge-check">
    You make it, it's yours to keep private, publish, or delete. Publishing
    shares it with the community — people can play it and
    [remix it](/discover/remix-apps) with automatic credit back to you. The
    fine print lives at [whip.run/terms](https://whip.run/terms).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I use an app someone shared without making my own stuff?" icon="link">
    Yes. During sign-up, tell Whip you're here to use apps rather than build
    them — the feed, search, and every shared link work the same. Most
    people end up building something eventually anyway. It's contagious.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What does 'remix' mean?" icon="shuffle">
    Remixing gives you your own editable copy of someone's public app — their
    design and features as your starting point, your chat to make it yours.
    The original creator gets credit automatically. [How remixing
    works](/discover/remix-apps).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Getting around

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I find good apps to play?" icon="compass">
    The [feed](/discover/the-feed) has a personalized **For You** tab and a
    **Trending** tab, plus category filters. There's also
    [search](/discover/search) for apps and creators, and a daily **App of
    the Day** notification worth keeping on.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I play with friends?" icon="users">
    Yes — share any app with a [link](/sharing/share-links), compete on
    [leaderboards](/using/play-together), or join a live
    [shared session](/using/play-together) where up to 10 people see the
    same data (shared lists, party scoreboards, trip planners).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Where do my apps live?" icon="house">
    On your [Home screen](/using/home-screen-and-folders) — every app you've
    made or saved, arrangeable like your phone's home screen, folders and
    all.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What's new in Whip lately?" icon="newspaper">
    Whip ships every week. The [changelog](/changelog) has the full history —
    recent highlights include multiplayer sessions, leaderboards, home-screen
    folders, and custom AI-designed app icons.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
